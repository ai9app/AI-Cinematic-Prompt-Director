## 🌟 About This Framework

A professional prompt engineering framework for AI video models (Sora, Kling, Seedance) featuring a strict **scene-by-scene video prompt structure** and a **250-item cinematography knowledge base**. Master camera movements, 3D design, and video effects to elevate your AI outputs to commercial grade.

AI video models require highly specific, visually grounded language to produce consistent results. This framework acts as your "Virtual Cinematographer," transforming vague ideas into structured prompt matrices by controlling:
*   **Camera Movements** (e.g., FPV Tracking, Dolly Zoom)
*   **Shot Types** (e.g., Extreme Close-Up, Dutch Angle)
*   **Lighting & Vibe** (e.g., Volumetric Lighting, Cyberpunk Neon)
*   **Visual Effects (VFX)** (e.g., Anamorphic lens flare, Film grain)
*   **Video Types** (e.g., Cinematic Trailers, TV Commercials)
